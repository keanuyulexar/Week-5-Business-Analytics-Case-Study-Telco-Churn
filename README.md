# 📊 Week 5 Business Analytics Case Study – Telco Customer Churn

## Overview

This project was completed as part of the **AnalystLab Africa Data Analytics Internship Program (Week 5)**.

The objective was to analyze customer churn data from a telecommunications company, identify the major factors contributing to customer churn, and provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn negatively impacts company revenue and profitability. The goal of this project was to determine why customers leave the company and identify opportunities to improve customer retention.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset:** Telco Customer Churn Dataset

- 7,043 customer records
- 21 features
- Customer demographics
- Service subscriptions
- Billing information
- Contract details
- Customer churn status

---

## Key Findings

- Customers on month-to-month contracts experienced the highest churn.
- Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to leave.
- Electronic check users showed higher churn.
- Fiber optic customers had higher churn than DSL customers.
- Higher monthly charges were associated with increased churn.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Promote automatic payment methods.
- Improve Fiber optic customer experience.
- Introduce customer loyalty programs.
